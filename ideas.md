**Approach A: "Texas Noir Luxury" — Dark Editorial Finance**

- **Design Movement**: Contemporary editorial luxury meets Texas boldness — think Architectural Digest meets Goldman Sachs dark mode
- **Core Principles**:
  1. Deep navy/charcoal backgrounds with razor-sharp white typography
  2. Asymmetric split layouts — text left, visual right, never centered
  3. Thin gold accent lines as structural dividers, not decoration
  4. Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) as typographic anchors
- **Color Philosophy**: Deep navy (#0A0F1E) base, off-white (#F5F0E8) text, Texas gold (#C9A84C) accents. Conveys wealth, trust, and authority without being stuffy.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Full-bleed horizontal strips with alternating content/image splits. Hero is a diagonal split — dark left with headline, photo right with subtle grain overlay.
- **Signature Elements**:
  1. Thin horizontal gold rule lines separating sections
  2. Large italic serif display font for hero headlines (Playfair Display)
  3. Monospace numbers for stats (18+, 500+, 24h)
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth parallax on hero image, cards lift with subtle shadow on hover, CTA buttons have a gold border sweep animation
- **Animation**: Staggered fade-up on scroll for all section content; hero text slides in from left; stats count up on viewport entry
- **Typography System**: Playfair Display (bold italic) for headlines + DM Sans for body. Hierarchy: 72px hero → 48px section → 24px sub → 16px body

**Approach B: "Kinetic Texas" — Bold Geometric Motion**

- **Design Movement**: Swiss International Typographic Style meets Texas energy — brutalist grid with warm Texas palette
- **Core Principles**:
  1. Oversized bold type as the primary visual element
  2. Hard geometric shapes (rectangles, diagonal cuts) as layout devices
  3. High contrast warm cream + deep forest green + burnt orange
  4. Motion as a core design element, not decoration
- **Color Philosophy**: Warm cream (#FAF7F2) base, forest green (#1B3A2D) primary, burnt orange (#D4622A) accent. Feels grounded, Texan, and energetic simultaneously.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Diagonal section cuts (clip-path) creating dynamic flow between sections. Stats section uses a full-bleed green band. Hero uses oversized stacked type.
- **Signature Elements**:
  1. Diagonal clip-path transitions between every section
  2. Oversized outlined text (text-stroke) for visual depth
  3. Rotating badge element (NMLS #114198 · Texas Licensed · Equal Housing)
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Magnetic hover on CTA buttons, loan cards flip to reveal details, process steps animate in sequence on scroll
- **Animation**: Hero headline splits word-by-word on load; section entries use a horizontal wipe; numbers roll up on scroll
- **Typography System**: Bebas Neue for display + Outfit for body. Creates maximum contrast between headline impact and readable body text.

## Selected Approach: **B — "Kinetic Texas"**

Bold, diagonal, energetic. Warm cream base with forest green and burnt orange. Bebas Neue headlines. Diagonal section cuts. This feels uniquely Texan and unmistakably modern — nothing like a typical mortgage site.
